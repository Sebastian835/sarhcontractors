Who We Are

Led on-site, not from behind a desk.

At SARH Contractors LLC, every remodeling project is a chance to turn a space into something functional, modern, and genuinely well built. Led by Santos Alexander Rivera Hernández, we've served homeowners and businesses across the area with professionalism, integrity, and close attention to detail.

From small repairs to complete home renovations, our team focuses on solid workmanship, efficient project management, and a level of communication that keeps you informed at every stage — because trust, clear communication, and craftsmanship are what a successful project is built on.
